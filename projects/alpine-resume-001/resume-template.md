## Template Usage Instructions

### Section Guidelines

**Contact Information:**
- Keep minimal and professional
- Include only methods you want potential employers to use
- Authorization statement is optional but recommended for international candidates

**Links:**
- Include professional profiles only (GitHub, LinkedIn, personal portfolio)
- Ensure all links are current and active
- Remove this section if you don't have professional online presence

**Summary:**
- Keep to 2-4 sentences maximum
- Focus on your unique value proposition
- Highlight years of experience and key specialties
- Make it compelling and specific

**Specialties:**
- Group by major competency areas (typically 2-5 areas)
- Each specialty should have a brief 1-2 sentence description
- Focus on breadth and depth of expertise
- Include certifications or key frameworks when relevant

**Work Experience:**
- List in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
- Use bullet points for accomplishments and responsibilities
- Start each bullet with an action verb (Led, Implemented, Developed, etc.)
- Quantify achievements when possible (e.g., "reduced testing time by 40%")
- Focus on impact and results, not just tasks
- Include company context (1-2 sentences about what the company does)
- If you held multiple roles at one company, separate them clearly
- Use horizontal rules (---) to separate positions for readability

**Education:**
- List in reverse chronological order
- Include degree, institution, field of study, location, and dates
- Include relevant certifications or continuing education
- If you didn't complete a degree, still list the coursework

**Skills:**
- Organize by category for easy scanning
- Include experience level in years for each skill
- Use "X+" notation for 10 or more years
- Be honest about experience levels
- Focus on skills relevant to your target roles
- Consider separating technical skills from soft skills

### Formatting Tips

- Use consistent formatting throughout
- Bold company names and role titles for easy scanning
- Use bullet points (- or •) for lists
- Keep line spacing consistent
- Use horizontal rules (---) to separate major sections within categories
- Ensure all dates follow the same format (MM/YYYY or Month Year)
- Keep descriptions concise and action-oriented

### Customization

This template is designed to be flexible. You can:
- Add or remove sections based on your experience
- Adjust skill categories to match your field
- Include additional sections like Publications, Awards, Certifications, etc.
- Modify the structure to emphasize your strengths
- Tailor content for specific job applications

### Length Considerations

- Entry-level: 1 page
- Mid-career (5-10 years): 1-2 pages
- Senior-level (10+ years): 2 pages
- Executive or extensive experience: 2-3 pages maximum

Remember: Quality over quantity. Every line should add value.
